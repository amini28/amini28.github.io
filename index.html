Our commitment to your privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how iOS Dev handles information in connection with our iOS applications available on the Apple App Store.

We build apps with a privacy-first approach. We collect only what is strictly necessary for the app to function, and we never sell your personal data.

Information we do not collect

Unless explicitly stated in an individual app's privacy policy, we do not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Track your location
  • Share or sell your data to third parties
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Store any data on our own servers

Data stored on your device

Most of our apps store data locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync within the app, in which case data is synced via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure and governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
