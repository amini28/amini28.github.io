Helikoptelor is a game. It does not collect user data or require network access during gameplay.

What this app collects

This app does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service used is Apple's own infrastructure (iCloud, CloudKit, StoreKit, etc.) where noted above, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data deletion

All app data stored on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also delete the associated iCloud data from Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage on your device.
